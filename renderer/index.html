I About InspireDesk

InspireDesk is your personal motivation companion that helps you stay focused on your daily goals by integrating them into your desktop environment.

How to Use:

  1. Enter your daily goals in the text area (one goal per line)
  2. Customize the appearance using Wallpaper Settings
  3. Click "Set Goals" to update your wallpaper
  4. Use "Load Today's Goals" to view or edit previous goals
  5. Edit or delete individual goals using the action buttons
